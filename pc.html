Minecraft Inventory Editor Help

Watch the Minecraft Inventory Editor Demo Video.

First, make sure you understand that this is BETA software... if you cherish your saved game, then back it up before you go messing with half-baked software.

Click the 'Upload Inventory...' button. You'll need to locate your saved game folder to locate the 'level.dat' file. Replace "Username" with your OS username.

Mac OS:/Users/Username/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves/

Windows:C:\Users\Username\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\saves\

Linux:/home/Username/.minecraft/saves/

After your inventory loads and you see your current inventory, you can drag items from the right set of tabs to your inventory slots.

Click 'Save Inventory' to download modified save file which you must use to overwrite your existing 'level.dat' with. Don't worry about the crazy filename, it's a unique identifier for your download!

Remember! Minecraft saves it's own emergency backup titled 'level.dat_old' - use it wisely!
